## WRITING GUIDELINES

### Structure Requirements
- Word Count: 1,200-2,000 words
- H1: Title with primary keyword
- H2: 4-6 major sections
- H3: Supporting points as needed
- Final: Conclusion with CTA

### Content Focus
1. **Specific Value:** Address one aspect thoroughly
2. **Answer Focus:** Directly answer the implied question
3. **Practical Tips:** Give actionable takeaways
4. **Relevance:** Connect clearly to reader's needs
5. **Pillar Connection:** Link back to main topic

### SEO Requirements
- Primary keyword in title
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- LSI keywords throughout (5-8 occurrences)
- Internal link to pillar content (required)
- Internal links to 1-2 related supporting articles
- External link to authoritative source (1-2)
- Local SEO integration where relevant
