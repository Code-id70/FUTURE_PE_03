## WRITING GUIDELINES

### Structure Requirements
- Word Count: 2,500-4,000 words
- H1: Main title with primary keyword
- H2: 6-10 major sections covering subtopics
- H3: Supporting points under each H2
- Final Section: Conclusion with CTA

### Content Requirements
1. **Comprehensive Coverage:** Address all aspects of the topic
2. **Expert Authority:** Demonstrate deep knowledge
3. **Practical Value:** Give actionable information
4. **Local Relevance:** Integrate location naturally
5. **Trust Building:** Include credentials and social proof

### SEO Requirements
- Primary keyword in title (H1)
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Primary keyword in meta description
- 2-3 keywords in H2 headings
- LSI keywords throughout (8-12 occurrences)
- Internal links to ALL supporting content
- External links to 2-3 authoritative sources
- Schema markup suggestions

### Formatting
- Prose-first approach (80%+ of content)
- Paragraphs: 2-4 sentences each
- Lists: Maximum 4 items, use sparingly
- Bold: Key terms (not for emphasis)
- Tables: For comparative data only
