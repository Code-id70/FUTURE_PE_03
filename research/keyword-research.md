## Keyword Intent Analysis

### Informational Keywords (Top of Funnel)

- Target: Building awareness, establishing authority
- Content: Blog posts, guides, FAQs
- Examples: "how to prevent cavities," "what causes gum disease"

### Commercial Investigation Keywords (Middle of Funnel)

- Target: Researching options before buying
- Content: Service pages, comparison guides
- Examples: "best dentist Johannesburg," "dental implants vs bridges"

### Transactional Keywords (Bottom of Funnel)

- Ready to purchase or book appointment
- Content: Service pages, contact CTAs
- Examples: "book dentist appointment," "dental implants price"

### Urgent Keywords

- Emergency situations
- Content: Emergency pages, immediate CTAs
- Examples: "emergency dentist open now," "toothache relief"
