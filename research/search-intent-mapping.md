## Understanding Search Intent

### The Four Types of Search Intent

**Informational (I):** Users seeking knowledge or answers to questions. Not ready to purchase but researching.

**Navigational (N):** Users looking for a specific website, brand, or location. Already know what they want.

**Commercial Investigation (C):** Users comparing options before making a purchase decision. In research mode.

**Transactional (T):** Users ready to take action, such as booking an appointment or making a purchase.

## Content-Intent Alignment by Funnel Stage

### Top of Funnel (Awareness)

**User Mindset:** "I have a problem or question. I want to learn more."

**Target Keywords:**

- what causes tooth sensitivity
- why are my gums bleeding
- how to prevent cavities
- what is a dental implant
- when should my child visit dentist

**Content Strategy:**

- Educational blog posts
- Comprehensive guides
- FAQ sections
- How-to articles

**Example Content:**

- "7 Common Causes of Tooth Sensitivity and How to Fix Them"
- "Everything You Need to Know About Dental Implants"
- "When Should Your Child First Visit the Dentist?"

### Middle of Funnel (Consideration)

**User Mindset:** "I'm exploring solutions. I want to compare options."

**Target Keywords:**

- dentist vs dental clinic
- dental implants vs bridges
- braces vs Invisalign
- professional whitening vs at-home
- best dentist in Johannesburg

**Content Strategy:**

- Comparison guides
- Service pages
- Pros and cons content
- Reviews and testimonials

**Example Content:**

- "Dental Implants vs. Bridges: Which Is Right for You?"
- "Invisalign vs. Traditional Braces: A Complete Comparison"
- "How to Choose the Best Dentist in Johannesburg"

### Bottom of Funnel (Decision)

**User Mindset:** "I'm ready to act. I want to book or buy."

**Target Keywords:**

- book dentist appointment Johannesburg
- dental implants cost
- emergency dentist near me
- teeth whitening price
- dentist accepting new patients

**Content Strategy:**

- Clear service pages
- Pricing information
- Easy booking options
- Contact CTAs
- Trust signals

**Example Content:**

- "Schedule Your Dental Implant Consultation Today"

## Intent Mismatch Analysis

### Common Intent Mistakes

| Keyword                 | Mistake                  | Corrected Approach           |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------ | ---------------------------- |
| dentist near me         | Optimizing for blog post | Optimizing for location page |
| how much do braces cost | Generic pricing          | Localized, detailed pricing  |
| emergency dentist       | Informational content    | Urgent-action page + phone   |
| dental implants         | Single procedure page    | Comprehensive treatment hub  |

### Why Intent Matching Matters

**Positive Signals for High Intent Match:**

- Lower bounce rate
- Higher time on page
- More pages per session
- Higher conversion rates
- Better engagement metrics

**Negative Signals for Intent Mismatch:**

- High bounce rate
- Low time on page
- High exit rate
- Low conversion rates
- Poor user experience
